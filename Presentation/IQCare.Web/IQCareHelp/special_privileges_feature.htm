Special Privileges Feature

  

There are two administrative options that have been designated as "Special Privileges". They are:

 

•Ability to edit the Patient Enrollment Number field

•Ability to edit the Care Tracking form

 

To give a user group one or both of these privileges, simply click on the check boxes next to the feature.
