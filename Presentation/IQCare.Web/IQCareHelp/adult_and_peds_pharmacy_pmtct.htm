Adult and Peds Pharmacy

  

The adult and paediatric pharmacy forms for PMTCT are the same as for the HIV Care program except you can only dispense ARVs for prophylaxis and not for treatment.

 

You must check the prophylaxis box in order to dispense ARVs and save the form.
